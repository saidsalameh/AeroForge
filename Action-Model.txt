# In this folder we describe the action model that will shape our
# training environment

Drone control model :
action = [collective_thrust, roll_rate, pitch_rate, yaw_rate]

A quadcopter drone have 6 degrees of freedom and 4 inputs.

Motion control determination :
1. Thrust 
2. Torque

Index  Name           Description
-----  -------------  -------------------------------------------
0      x              Position in world X (meters)
1      y              Position in world Y (meters)
2      z              Position in world Z (meters, altitude)

3      vx             Linear velocity along X (m/s)
4      vy             Linear velocity along Y (m/s)
5      vz             Linear velocity along Z (m/s)

6      roll           Roll angle about X (rad)
7      pitch          Pitch angle about Y (rad)
8      yaw            Yaw angle about Z (rad)

9      p              Angular rate around X (rad/s)
10     q              Angular rate around Y (rad/s)
11     r              Angular rate around Z (rad/s)

12     dx             x - x_target (position error in X)
13     dy             y - y_target (position error in Y)
14     dz             z - z_target (position error in Z)
